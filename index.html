About Us

AmpLuxe Energy Solutions LLP provides comprehensive and reliable solar and electrical services to power a sustainable future for commercial and residential clients. Based in Mount Road, Chennai, we specialise in end-to-end energy solutions ensuring safe, compliant and optimised installations.

With over 20 years of expertise in this field we aim to provide the best service we have to offer.

Our Core Services

Drive your energy future forward with flexible, customised managed services and support

Solar Panel Systems Installation

Our end-to-end Solar Panel System Installation service offers energy freedom. We design, install, and secure permits for custom systems that suit your energy needs and roof layout. Generate your own power and enjoy the peace of mind of a sustainable, long-term investment for residential, commercial office spaces, and large commercial buildings.

Electrical Wiring Services

Reliable Power, Perfect Safety. Our Electrical Wiring Services ensure your building's wiring is safe and efficient, whether you're building new or renovating. Our certified electricians use high-grade, compliant materials to install robust wiring that meets all safety standards, supporting modern power demands.

Meter Box Installation & Upgrades

Is your old meter box a risk or a bottleneck? We offer expert meter box installation and upgrades to safely manage your property's power intake. This is crucial for handling the load of new air conditioners, electric vehicle chargers, or a new solar system. We ensure your meter is correctly rated, compliant, and ready to safely deliver all the power you need, today and tomorrow.

New Electrical Connection Support & Installation

We simplify the process of getting your power connected when building a new home or business. We handle all the paperwork and technical work, from applying for your connection to the final installation and safety check. We ensure your home or business is powered up quickly and stress-free.

Maintenance Works for all systems

Protect your energy investment. Our comprehensive Maintenance Works cover routine inspections, preventative care, performance checks, and rapid repairs for all our installed systems. A small investment in maintenance today ensures maximum efficiency, safety, and system longevity, maximising your savings.
